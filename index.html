About Me

Dr. Mitchell L. Doucette is a seasoned public health researcher with extensive expertise in injury epidemiology, firearms policy, and medical cannabis. As the Senior Research Director at Leafwell and a Faculty Associate at Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health, he leads complex, mission-driven research on health outcomes and policy, with a focus on causal inference and epidemiological methods. His work, widely published and featured in major media outlets, has significantly impacted public health and policy, including citations in landmark legal decisions.
