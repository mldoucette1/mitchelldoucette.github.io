About Me

Dr. Mitchell L. Doucette is a seasoned public health researcher with expertise in injury epidemiology, firearms policy, and medical cannabis. As Senior Research Director at Leafwell and Faculty Associate at Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health, he leads research on causal inference and health outcomes. His work is widely published and has been cited in landmark legal cases and public health policy.
